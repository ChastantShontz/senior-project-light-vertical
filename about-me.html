A little about me...

Great to "see" you here! I built this website for my senior capstone, hopefully it's not TOO bad...


I've lived in Saratoga Springs for my whole life, until going off to college. My family includes my parents, my little brother, and all four of my dogs. Sparkles, my Australian Shepard/English Springer Spaniel mix, is definitely my favorite. My dad lives in Greenwich with his wife, so I'm over there visiting them all the time, too. For all of primary and secondary school, I attended the Waldorf School of Saratoga Springs.


I'm probably the biggest Star Wars fan you'll ever meet (this will quickly become apparent when you take a look at some of the websites I've built!), which I find ironic since I was actually born on Star Wars Day. A close second favorite of mine would have to be Arrow. I also love parkour, skiing, and writing, having written a full-length novel for my high school senior project. Someday, I'd like to turn it into a whole series. My favorite foods are ice cream and literally anything Chinese or Japanese.


In 2020, at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, I graduated high school. At first, I wanted to be a creative writing major, but I ended up going to Clarkson University and majoring in communications instead. It was here that I discovered a passion for web development and all things coding, which is the field that I'd like to go into. I'm in the honors program here as well as the pep band, where I play the cowbell off-beat at all our hockey games (LET'S GO TECH!!!).


I've had several on-campus jobs at Clarkson, such as an intern at the Shipley Center for Innovation and a tour guide with the admissions office. During breaks, I work at Stewart's Shops near my home. This past summer, I was fortunate enough to be offered an internship as a financial advisor at Northwestern Mutual. I'm now on the hunt for a job in my chosen field, so I can finally put this college degree to some good use.


And now you know a little about me. If you think we'd be a good fit to work together, go ahead and check out some of my past work or shoot me a message!
